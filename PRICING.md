# Layoutr Pricing

## Credit System

| Unit | Value |
|------|-------|
| 1 credit | $0.0001 |
| 1,000 credits | $2.00 |

Credits are consumed per AI generation call, based on actual token usage (input + output) with a 2× markup over raw API cost.

### Credit cost per generation (estimates)

| Model | Typical | Heavy |
|-------|---------|-------|
| DeepSeek V4 Flash | ~50 credits ($0.10) | ~100 credits ($0.20) |
| Claude Sonnet 4.5 | ~500 credits ($1.00) | ~1,000 credits ($2.00) |
| GPT-5.5 | ~2,500 credits ($5.00) | ~5,000 credits ($10.00) |

## Plans

### Free
**$0/mo**

- 100 credits/month included
- DeepSeek V4 Flash only
- Max additional credit purchase: 500 credits/mo ($1)
- 1 project

---

### Hobby
**$9/mo**

- 1,000 credits/month included
- DeepSeek V4 Flash + Claude Sonnet 4.5
- Max additional credit purchase: 5,000 credits/mo ($10)
- Unlimited projects

---

### Pro
**$29/mo**

- 5,000 credits/month included
- All models (DeepSeek, Claude Sonnet, GPT-5.5)
- Max additional credit purchase: 50,000 credits/mo ($100)
- Unlimited projects
- Priority support

---

### Agency
**$99/mo**

- 25,000 credits/month included
- All models
- Max additional credit purchase: 500,000 credits/mo ($1,000)
- Unlimited projects
- Priority support
- Early access to new features

---

## Credit Packs (add-on, within plan limit)

| Pack | Price | Savings |
|------|-------|---------|
| 1,000 credits | $2 | — |
| 5,000 credits | $8 | save $2 |
| 20,000 credits | $28 | save $12 |

Credit packs can only be purchased up to the monthly cap for your plan. Unused purchased credits roll over; monthly included credits reset each billing cycle.
